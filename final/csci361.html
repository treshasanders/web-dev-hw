ATM Architecture Presentation

CSCI 361: Computer Architecture

Assignment Description

For the final project for the class, we were to find a partner and work together to create a presentation discussing the computer architecture of a topic we decided on.

What I Learned

We decided to present on the architecture of an ATM Machine. I learned the extent of protection ATM's hardware contains to keep out potential thieves and hackers. From physical barriers to cryptoprocessors encrypting personal information, ATM's have very complex and intricate architectures. I created diagrams to aid the audience in visualizing the architecture. This was a very fun project I'm incredibly proud of.
