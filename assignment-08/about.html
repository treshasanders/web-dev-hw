About Tresha's Candles

Why I Chose This Project

For a very long time I told myself that my adult hobby would be soap making. By 'adult hobby', I mean a hobby I obtain when I'm out of college and I no longer feel like I'm living on borrowed time. Soap making seemed like a relaxing and fun option, but at some point I realized that soapmaking wasn't quite right. I don't use a lot of hard soaps. But I love candles.


What This Project Means to Me

Since I've decided that I want candle making to be my adult hobby, I needed to learn how to make candles! This was the perfect opportunity.
